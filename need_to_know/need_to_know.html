about the game

1. Playable character Pepe

Pepe, the brave Mexican hero from San Miguel, proudly wears his poncho and faces the wild chickens. They attack him, but Pepe deftly jumps on them and defeats one rooster after another. But the crazy chicken Coco is persistent. Pepe throws hot sauce bottles, but Coco narrowly escapes. As the sun slowly sets, Pepe stands there panting and looks resolutely at Coco. Destiny hangs in the air and the village of San Miguel hopes for Pepe's victory. The question remains open: Will Pepe finally defeat Coco and save the village?

2. Little enemy: The chicken

The chickens are small and inconspicuous, but don't underestimate their abilities. They are equipped with chicks that can jump amazingly high. The chicks follow the hens closely as they swarm around in wild confusion. The chickens themselves may not look strong, but their attacks are fast and incessant. They try to circle Pepe and attack from afar. But Pepe is not easy to defeat.

3. Endboss: El Pollo Loco

Loca, the crazy chicken, towers above the rest of the flock of chickens. With its impressive size, it towers head and shoulders above all other chickens. Her imposing physique is covered in muscular feathers that give her an imposing appearance. Loca's presence is almost menacing as she stands with spread wings and flaunts her imposing body weight. This impressive stature gives her a dominant air that makes her even more difficult to subdue. Pepe faces this giantess.

items

The coin

In Pepe's World you collect shiny coins that make Pepe faster. With each coin he feels more agile and moves faster through the colorful world. Use the coins to support Pepe on his way to defeating Loca the crazy chicken.

The salsa bottle

This legendary hot sauce bottle gives Pepe the strength he needs to defeat Loca the crazy chicken, he can find and collect them along the way and then throw them at Loca at the end!
